# ⚡ Boiler Controller

> **Turn your electric boiler into a smart thermal battery.**

The **Boiler Controller (BC)** transforms an ordinary electric boiler into a fully-fledged smart heat battery. It reads the surplus from your electric meter in real time and modulates the boiler accordingly: when you produce more than you consume, that energy goes straight into the heater instead of back to the grid.

The result: free solar and cheap dynamic-tariff energy stored as hot water — with no extra battery hardware.

---

## 🔋 Make your boiler a smart home battery

Paired with **Powerbaas**, **Home Assistant**, or **Domoticz**\*, the Boiler Controller steers your boiler fully automatically based on solar surplus or the lowest electricity prices. You save directly on your energy bill **and** relieve pressure on the grid.

---

## 💡 What does the Boiler Controller do for you?

By integrating with your real-time energy data, water heating becomes fully automated:

- **☀️ Use your solar surplus** — Producing more than you consume? That free energy flows straight into your smart heat battery instead of back to the grid.
- **💶 Profit from low electricity prices** — On a dynamic tariff? The system knows exactly when prices are lowest (or even negative) and switches the boiler on at those moments.
- **🌱 Increase self-consumption** — Less export, more of your own clean energy actually used at home. Smarter and more sustainable.

---

## 🧩 One compact controller. Endless possibilities.

The Boiler Controller is designed for **maximum flexibility**. No closed ecosystem — full freedom to build the smart setup you want.

| Capability | Details |
|---|---|
| 🔌 **Connectivity** | Read data directly via **Modbus** or **Pulse input** |
| ⚙️ **Control** | Switch heavy loads via **SSR** or modulate precisely with the **0–10V output** |
| 🌡️ **Monitoring** | Real-time monitoring with **temperature sensor** + seamless **Home Assistant** integration |

Whatever your setup, the Boiler Controller has the interface you need.

---

## 🛠️ How do you use it?

Today, the Boiler Controller works with **Powerbaas** and **Home Assistant**. You define your own rules and automations, such as:

- 🔥 Only heat when the electricity price is below 15 cents
- ☀️ Use solar surplus to heat water
- 🕖 Make sure the water is up to temperature every morning

**Coming soon:**
- ✅ **Domoticz** support
- 📱 A **dedicated app** for direct control — no third-party home-automation platform required

In this phase, the Boiler Controller is a **DIY solution**: you install it yourself and integrate it with your existing setup, with full freedom to automate it your way.
